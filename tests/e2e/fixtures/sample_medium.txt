Chapter One: The Beginning

Once upon a time, in a land far away, there lived a young programmer who dreamed of building great software.
Every day, she would sit at her desk and write code, testing each function carefully.
She believed that good tests were the foundation of reliable software.

Chapter Two: The Challenge

One day, a great challenge appeared before her. The audio conversion pipeline was broken.
Users could not convert their documents to audiobooks. The error messages were cryptic.
She decided to investigate the root cause systematically.

Chapter Three: The Solution

After careful analysis, she discovered that the database constraints were being violated.
The chunks were being inserted before the parent job record existed.
By reordering the operations, she fixed the foreign key constraint violation.
The pipeline worked correctly once more, and users could convert their documents.

Chapter Four: The Tests

To prevent future regressions, she wrote comprehensive end-to-end tests.
These tests used real files and exercised the complete pipeline.
They caught bugs that unit tests could never find.
The software became more reliable with each passing day.
